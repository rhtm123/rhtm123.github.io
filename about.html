Hi, I’m Rohit Maurya, an Educator, an Entrepreneur, and a Web Developer.



My Story

I was born and bought up in a small village of Sant Ravidas Nagar Bhadohi District of Uttar Pradesh. I attended one of the premier institutes of India. I learned Python and website development in college. I joined a company as a backend web developer and left the job after a few months. I decided never to join any company in the future.


Why I left the Job?

I don’t know the purpose of my life but I am entitled to have a successful life. A joyful and meaningful life is a success for me. A mentally and physically fit body is a success for me. Adding some positive values to other’s life is a meaningful life for me. I can create more joy and meaning in my life by working for myself rather working for someone else.


Why did I choose to be an educator?

I was a Guru from childhood. I always used to teach my fellow students and they liked my teaching style. Education was always a passion for me. I rediscover this passion after I left the job. Education is the perfect field for me as I enjoy teaching and It also adds the positive values to other’s life. I founded Gurukul.Guru to redefine education in India. I feel motivated when I am working on a challenging problem. Redefining the Indian Education System is indeed very challenging. Why the current education system needs redefinition?
